# Opt-outs

Public, append-only list of people who have asked not to be simulated by `/mask`.

If a person publicly requests that they not be simulated by LLM-driven persona tools,
add them here, set `status: retired` on any existing brief, and never re-add them.

## How an opt-out works

- A public statement (blog post, tweet, email response, etc.) saying "don't simulate me"
  or equivalent is sufficient.
- Cite the source so future maintainers can verify.
- Once on this list, do not remove the entry without an equally explicit public reversal
  from the same person.
